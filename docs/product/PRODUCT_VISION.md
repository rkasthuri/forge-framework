# FORGE Product Vision

# FORGE — Autonomous Quality Engineering

## Mission

Enable enterprise teams to onboard any application and rapidly create, execute, maintain, and continuously improve automated quality coverage with minimal human effort.

FORGE exists to transform Quality Engineering from a largely manual discipline into an intelligent, continuously learning platform.

---

# Vision Statement

A user should be able to point FORGE at any application and say:

"Learn this application and generate reliable automated coverage."

FORGE should then:

1. Discover the application.
2. Understand its structure.
3. Verify discovered behavior.
4. Generate production-quality tests.
5. Execute continuously.
6. Heal failures automatically.
7. Learn from every run.
8. Surface actionable insights.

---

# Core Product Principles

## Truth over optimism

FORGE must always communicate uncertainty.

Low confidence should never be hidden.

Unknowns should be surfaced rather than guessed.

---

## Enterprise reliability first

Reliability, auditability, and explainability outweigh speed.

Enterprise users must understand:

* what happened
* why it happened
* what evidence supports the conclusion

---

## Human-in-the-loop

AI accelerates Quality Engineering.

Humans remain final decision makers.

Generated artifacts are reviewed before promotion.

---

## Continuous learning

Every execution should improve future executions.

Generate → Execute → Heal → Learn

is a continuous cycle, not a one-time workflow.

# Reporting Vision

FORGE reporting is not a dashboard of charts.

The platform must allow users to move seamlessly from:

"Pass rate dropped"

to

"This element failed in Chrome for Admin role because selector X changed and has healed four times in the last two weeks."

without leaving the platform.

Drill-down and explainability are first-class capabilities.
